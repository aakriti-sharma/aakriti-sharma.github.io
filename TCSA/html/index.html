Tagore Cultural and Social Association


Update regarding Durga Puja 2020

Tagore Cultural and Social Association, Rajiv Nagar, Nashik, Estd. In 2015 with a handful of members and today we are more than 100 members to our list and still growing. It had been our dream to Celebrate Durga Puja and other Puja in a very Organized and befitting manner and now we are very proud to inform you that our Puja is visited by all in and around Nashik with a footfall of around 1000 devotees / day. This is because we have kept our hearts open for all and display our most hospitable approach for all. Besides, we have undertaken various social activities like distribution of books to needy, helping Blinds, Distribution of clean drinking water etc to a great mass of people and will continue to do so for the years ahead and something to ponder upon for our next Zen. Further to Puja, we also hold various Cultural activities, distribution of Mahaprasad and various entertainment activities to mark the occasion besides release of a beautiful and informative Souvenir which is distributed to over 1000 Industries in and around Nashik. All above are supported by a great and dedicated group of sponsors, our own members and other devotees and wellwishers who not only display their presence but also become a part of all activities. We appeal all to come forward and become our part to celebrate the Puja in a very significant and elegant manner.
